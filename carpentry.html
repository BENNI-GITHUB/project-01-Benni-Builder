Carpentry
Services

Benni Builder provides a wide range of carpentry services to enhance the functionality, aesthetics, and organization of your residential or commercial space. Our skilled carpenters possess expertise in various carpentry projects, including doors, floors, stairs, cabinets, wardrobes, drawers, and cupboards. With an emphasis on precision, craftsmanship, and attention to detail, we deliver exceptional results tailored to your specific needs.

Our Carpentry Services

Kitchen Cabinets

Our carpentry expertise extends to designing, building, and installing cabinets for kitchens, bathrooms, offices, and other spaces. We understand the importance of functional and visually appealing cabinetry. Our carpenters utilize quality materials, advanced hardware, and custom design options to create cabinets that maximize storage space while complementing your overall design aesthetic.

Doors

We offer professional door installation and repair services to upgrade the entrances and interior doors of your property. Whether you need standard doors, custom-designed doors, or specialized doors for specific purposes (such as fire-rated doors or soundproof doors), our carpenters ensure proper fitting, smooth operation, and attractive finishes.

Floor and Stairs

Benni Builder specializes in flooring and stair installation and refinishing services. Whether you prefer hardwood, laminate, tile, or any other flooring material, our carpenters can handle the installation with precision and skill. Additionally, we construct and repair staircases, ensuring safety, durability, and aesthetic appeal.

Wardrobes, Drawers and Cupboards

If you require customized storage solutions, we offer the construction and installation of wardrobes, drawers, and cupboards. Our carpenters can design and build these pieces to fit your specific requirements, optimizing space utilization and providing organization solutions tailored to your needs. We combine functionality with style to create storage units that enhance the overall aesthetics of your space.
