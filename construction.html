Building Repair &
Construction Services

Benni Builder provides a wide range of carpentry services to enhance the functionality, aesthetics, and organization of your residential or commercial space. Our skilled carpenters possess expertise in various carpentry projects, including doors, floors, stairs, cabinets, wardrobes, drawers, and cupboards. With an emphasis on precision, craftsmanship, and attention to detail, we deliver exceptional results tailored to your specific needs.

Our Carpentry Services

Painting and Decorating

Our skilled painters and decorators can revitalize your property's interior and exterior with precision and attention to detail. We provide professional painting services, including surface preparation, color selection, and flawless paint application. Whether you need a fresh coat of paint, textured finishes, or decorative painting techniques, we ensure a beautiful and long-lasting result.

Plastering Services

Our plastering services cover both repair and installation. Whether your walls have cracks, dents, or other damages, our skilled plasterers can restore them to their original condition. We also specialize in plasterboard installation and can create smooth, even surfaces for your walls and ceilings. Our goal is to provide a seamless finish that enhances the overall aesthetics of your space.

Kitchen and Bathroom Tile

Transform your kitchen and bathroom with our professional tile installation services. Our experienced tilers are skilled in working with various tile materials, including ceramic, porcelain, marble, and mosaic. We ensure precise measurements, proper adhesive application, and meticulous grouting to create stunning and functional tiled surfaces. Whether it's kitchen backsplashes, bathroom floors, or shower enclosures, we bring expertise to every tile installation project.
